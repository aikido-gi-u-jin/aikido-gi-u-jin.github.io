Is Aikido For Me? Try It!

Aikido a Japanese martial art for self defence. We use a variety of locks and throws to control our attackers but unlike the better-known Japanese art of Judo we stay on our feet and don't wrestle on the mat. It is great for women and men of all ages and we work in small practice groups so everyone can learn at their own pace. Your first class is free. Please Email us if you would like to give it a try.

Watch A Class

This video was taken at a class in May by one of our members. We started, as always, a warm up and then we moved on to a Ki development exercise called Koho Tento Undo (rolling exercise). After a while we broke up into groups and spent the rest of the class working on our techniques for the grading which was a couple of weeks away. Finally we finished by showing respect and gratitude to each other by shaking hands and thanking one another for the practice.
